The Years

A short record of what we attempted, what worked, and what we learned. First‑person, intentionally. Limited production, unlimited cleanup.

October 2021 — First harvest

In 2021, Alicia and I were on a wine tour in Alba. We came home to the Ranch Zinfandel and decided to try making our own wine. We bought a crusher. We guessed. We learned.

October 2022 — Harvest Party

We tried to get fancy and hosted a harvest party. The harvest was a bit of a bust. The party was not.

October 2023 — Spain

We guessed harvest wrong and were in Spain during October. The vineyard did not adjust its schedule. We did the next year.

2024 — Breakout

First year collaborating with my friend and neighbor, Kevin. Together we made Tempranillo, Sangiovese, Sauvignon Blanc, and turned the Ranch Zin into sparkling wine. The winemaker’s blend was PTSG: Pinot / Tempranillo / Sangiovese / Grenache.

2025 — In barrel (and in progress)

Still aging, of course. Tempranillo and Sauvignon Blanc again. 24 gallons of saignée from the Tempranillo. Rosé of Zin from the Ranch grapes. Sparkling is underway again.

The Work

Punchdowns, press days, racking days, cleanup. Repeat. Most of the year is this part.
