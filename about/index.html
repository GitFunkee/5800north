About

A private label,
made to be shared.

Small lots, careful technique, and a preference for restraint—in the cellar and on the label.

The name

“5800 North” is our address on Highway 116 in Forestville. “North” is also a quiet reference to Polaris—visible from the vineyard at night.

The approach

If it doesn’t help the wine, we try not to do it. Clean handling, deliberate choices, and clarity over fireworks.
